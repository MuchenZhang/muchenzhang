Cheese breads that look like fake croissants

  • Back

      • Ingredients

    • 450g all purpose flour
    • 7g dry yeast (Don't care if you use active or instant dry yeast)
    • Some sugar and salt
    • 260g milk
    • 50g melted butter
    • Dry mozzarella cheese of any fillings you desire
    • egg wash (optional)

      Process

    1. Mix up the flour, yast, sugar and salt, milk, and melted butter
    2. Knead the dough until it doesn't stick (might take 4 minutes)
    3. Let the dough rise for 1 to 2 hours, or until doubled in size
    4. No need to deflate it, devide the dough into 10 equal portions
    5. Just watch the video below, I can't explain
    6. Bake in a 180 ℃ oven for 15 to 18 minutes


      Things to read before you start making this recipe

      The dough should be easy to work with, cause of the butter inside. It shouldn't be too sticky. Other than that, everything is well explained in the video.

      Try different fillings such as chocolate or add in some cheese and pepperoni, that will make it a fake pizza
