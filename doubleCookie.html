2-in-1 Cookie

  • Back

      • Ingredients

    • 100g unsalted butter, softened
    • 130g granulated sugar
    • 2 eggs
    • 200g flour
    • 2 tsp baking powder
    • 2 tbsp cocoa powder
    • Granulated sugar for decuration

      Process

    1. Cream butter and sugar, then add in the eggs and mix well.
    2. Remove 2 tbsp of flour, set it in another bowl, then add the remaining flour and baking powder to butter mixture and mix until incorporated.
    3. Divide the cookie dough into two bowls, in one bowl, stir in the cocoa powder and to the other half fold in the couple tablepsoons of flour we reserved.
    4. Rest two doughs in the fridge for 15 minutes.
    5. Preheat oven to 190℃.
    6. "Using a small ice cream scoop, scoop out and roll all the dough (the chocolate and original) then take one of each, attach them together and roll around in your hand to combine them (watch video for clear instructions) then dip just the top half in the pearled or granulated sugar and place them a couple inches apart on your prepared baking sheet."
    7. Bake for 8 to 10 minutes.

      Things to read before you start making this recipe

      This recipe was developed by Laura Vitali at laurainthekitchen.com

      Add enough sugar into the dough, or you'll be left with a chewy, doughy, flavorless cookie.
