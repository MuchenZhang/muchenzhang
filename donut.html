Churro Donuts

  • Back

      • Ingredients

    • All Purpose Flour 280g
    • Sugar 30g
    • Some salt
    • Cinnamon powder 1 tbsp
    • 1 egg
    • Milk about 120mL
    • Melted butter 40g
    • Dry yeast 7g
    • Some granulated sugar for coating
    • A few tbsps of cinnamon powder for coating

      Process

    1. Combine all the ingredients for the dough and knead until smooth. This dough is quite sticky, so it might take a while if you are not using a mixer.
    2. Cover the dough and let rise until doubled in size.
    3. Once risen, roll out the dough on a floured surface to about 0.7cm thick. Cut out the donut shape with any tools.
    4. Cover the donuts for 20 minutes.
    5. Mix up granulated sugar and cinnamon for the churro coating.
    6. Fry the donuts at 160℃ to get a golden color. I used corn oil.
    7. Once the donuts have a nice color, coat them immediately with the churro coating mixture.
    8. Serve!

      Things to read before you start making this recipe

      The kneading process is the hardest, but you will get a super soft and fluffy donut in the end.

      Donut holes are the best, period.
