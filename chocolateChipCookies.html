Muchen's Chocolate Chip Cookies

  • Back

      • Ingredients

    • 1 egg
    • 2 tbsp coconut oil
    • 100g granulated sugar
    • 100g brown sugar
    • 250g flour
    • 2 tsp baking powder or baking soda, doesn't matter (optional)
    • some chocolate chips
    • a pinch of salt (optional)
    • 1 tsp vanilla extract (optional)

      Process

    1. Mix up the egg, coconut oil, and two types of sugar. Please crack the egg with one hand or else you are not eligible make these delicious cookies
    2. Add in the flour and baking powder/soda
    3. Add a pinch of salt if you don't want super sweet cookies
    4. Mix them up util you get a dough-like consistency
    5. Preheat your oven to 180 ℃
    6. Since this recipe is super forgiving, you don't need to chill your dough. Just add a bit more flour if it's too runny
    7. Make little dough balls with your hands (or spoons or an ice cream scooper if you wanna be fancy like those people in Tasty)
    8. Flatten the dough balls a bit
    9. Bake them in the oven for about 15 to 20 minutes or until golden brown

      Things to read before you start making this recipe

      There are so many variations to this recipe. You can substitute the coconut oil for butter or vegetable oil. Just remember to put some fat in there. If you want a healthier version of this recipe, simply replace flour with almond flour or coconut flour.

      You can always cut or add 30% of the sugar in this recipe, depending on how sweet you want it to be. And do not worry if you don't have brown sugar, putting only granulated sugar will also work, the cookie will just be crispier.

      There are two types of people when it comes to cookies, the crispy people and the chewy people. Personally, I like a crispier cookie, but you can also add more brown sugar to make it more chewy.

      You can put either dark chocolate or mild chocolate into your cookie. I prefer darker chocolate just because it balances the sweetness from all those sugar. But whatever works for you.
