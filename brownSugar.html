Brown Sugar Buns

  • Back

      • Ingredients

    • 500g flour
    • 4g salt
    • 300mL water
    • Some vegetable oil
    • 4 tbsp brown sugar
    • 1 tbsp flour

      Process

    1. Form the first three ingredients to a dough, cover and rest for one hour.
    2. Mix brown sugar and flour, the ratio is 4:1.
    3. Divide the dough into eight portions.
    4. For each "small dough", roll out to a flat circle and place some brown sugar mixture in the middle.
    5. In an oiled pan, place the bun over medium heat for 2 to 3 minutes on each side or until golden brown. Keep the lid on while cooking.

      Things to read before you start making this recipe

      You can add in yeast to the dough for a fluffier bun. But it'll still taste good without the yeast.
