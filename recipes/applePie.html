Apple Pie

  • Back

      • Ingredients

    • Crust: butter 60g
    • Crust: flour 120g
    • Crust: half an egg (save other half for egg wash)
    • Filling: 3 apples, peeled and chopped into cubes
    • Filling: sugar 50g
    • Filling: butter 20g
    • Filling: cinnamon 1 tsp

      Process

    1. Cut the butter into small cubes and add it in to the dry ingredients.
    2. Pinch the butter into smaller pieces with your fingertips and rub them into the flour.
    3. Add half the egg and mix, save some for the eggwash later.
    4. The dough should come together pretty easily. Wrap and chill in the fridge.
    5. Meanwhile, melt butter in a pan and add in the ingredients for the filling.
    6. Caramelize the apples.
    7. Roll out the dough into the size of your container and lay it over the bottom.
    8. Add in the apple filling.
    9. Create designs on top of the pie, brush on some egg wash.
    10. Bake at 180℃ for 40 minutes.

      Things to read before you start making this recipe

      Use only the egg yolk if you want a darker color on your pie.

      I don't recommend doing very complicated designs with this pie recipe because the dough can be pretty hard to work with.
