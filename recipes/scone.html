Scones

  • Back

      • Ingredients

    • Flour 210g
    • Sugar 50g
    • Baking powder 8g
    • Cold butter 55g
    • Milk 85mL
    • 1 egg

      Process

    1. Cut the butter into small cubes and add it in to the dry ingredients.
    2. Pinch the butter into smaller pieces with your fingertips and rub them into the flour.
    3. Add half the egg and mix, save some for the eggwash later.
    4. Add in the milk gradually until it can roughly form a dough.
    5. Don't knead the dough, just bring it together on a floured surface.
    6. Roll out the dough to about 2.5cm thick.
    7. Cut into little circles and brush on the egg saved earlier.
    8. Bake at 180℃ for 20-22 minutes.

      Things to read before you start making this recipe

      Use only the egg yolk if you want a darker color on your scones.
