Cream Puffs 2.0

  • Back

      • Ingredients

    • Water 100mL
    • Butter 60g
    • Flour 60g
    • 3 to 4 eggs
    • Pastry Cream (See "Banana Cream Pie" for how to make custard)
    • Powdered sugar for decoration

      Process

    1. Melt butter in water, bring the mixture to a boil.
    2. Turn off the heat, sift in flour and mix.
    3. Mix until you see a thin film formed on the bottom of the pan
    4. Cool the mixture a bit.
    5. Add in the eggs one by one, you should reach a consistency where the mixture forms a "V" shape after falling from the spatula.
    6. Pipe it on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Pipe them BIG, around 5cm in diameter.
    7. Bake at a preheated oven at 190℃ for 30 minutes.
    8. Once the cream puffs come out of the oven, cool it down and pipe in whipped cream or custard. Add decoration.

      Things to read before you start making this recipe

      I made some changes in the ingredients this time. In my last cream puff recipe, I used milk and got a darker on top. In this recipe, I used water instead. Water makes the cream puffs crispier and "stand" longer.

      You can also make Eclairs using this dough, they bake at differenc temperatures, but the general idea is the same.
