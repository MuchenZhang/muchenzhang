One Pan Pasta For One

  • Back

      • Ingredients

    • Half an onion, chopped
    • Olive oil
    • 3 mushrooms
    • 1 leftover hotdog from breakfast
    • pasta
    • 1 tsp tomato paste
    • water
    • salt and pepper

      Process

    1. Preheat the pan and drizzle in some olive oil.
    2. Saute the onion until softened.
    3. Add in sliced mushrooms and the hotdog. Cook until the mushrooms turn soft and brown.
    4. Add in water until it covers the top of the ingredients.
    5. Bring water to a boil, then add in the pasta.
    6. Add in a tsp of tomato paste.
    7. Cook the pasta for 10 to 12 minutes till al dente. Don't put on the pan lid, we are reducing the water.
    8. If there is not much water left when the pasta is still hard, add more water. The starch in the pasta thickens the sauce.
    9. Serve with some chopped scallions.

      Things to read before you start making this recipe

      You get the general idea, you can through in anything you have in the pot and it will taste good.

      Season as you go, but the most important thing is to taste from time to time. This dish is very easy to overseason.
