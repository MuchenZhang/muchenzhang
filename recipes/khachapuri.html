Khachapuri

  • Back

      • Ingredients

    • Flour 300g
    • Water 200mL
    • Salt 2g
    • Yeast 4g
    • Cheese 200g, I used frozen mozzarella for pizzas
    • 1 egg

      Process

    1. Combine flour, water, salt, and yeast. Knead to a dough. Always add more flour if the dough is too sticky.
    2. Cover and rest the dough for one hour. I didn't wait that long because I'm impatient.
    3. Once the dough is risen, roll out to a rectangle/square.
    4. Place two rows of cheese, one near the top of the square, and one near the bottom.
    5. Roll the top and bottom up so that the two rows of cheese get enclosed by the dough. These will be the borders of the "boat".
    6. Twist and pinch the ends.
    7. Add some more cheese in the "boat".
    8. Preheat oven to 250℃ for 15 minutes.
    9. Bake for 15 minutes.
    10. Take the khachapuri out and crack an egg.
    11. Bake in the oven for annother 3 to 4 minutes until the white is cooked and the yold is a bit runny.
    12. Sprinkle on some salt.
    13. Dig in!
