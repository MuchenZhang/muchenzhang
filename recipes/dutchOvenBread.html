Dutch oven bread

  • Back

      • Ingredients

    • Water 500mL
    • Yeast 4g
    • Flour 1000g
    • Salt 6g

      Process

    1. Dissolve yeast in water, then add in flour and salt. Mix with spatula until no dry flour can be seen.
    2. Cover and rest for 1.5 hours.
    3. Deflate and fold the dough several times with a spatula in the same bowl.
    4. Cover and rest for another 1.5 hours.
    5. Transfer to a floured surface and shape into a ball.
    6. Put the dough ball into an oiled, floured bowl and rest for 45 minutes.
    7. Meanwhile, preheat the oven with a dutch oven inside at 230℃ for 45 minutes.
    8. You would score the bread at this step.
    9. Take out the dutch oven and transfer the dough inside. CAUTION, it is very hot.
    10. Bake with the lid on for 30 minutes and another 15 minutes without the lid.
    11. Cool completely before serving.

      Things to read before you start making this recipe

      Be very CAREFUL when dealing with the dutch oven, I burnt a piece of my skin on the arm when taking it out.

      You don't have to knead the dough at all! All the steps can be done in one bowl.
