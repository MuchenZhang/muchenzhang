Homemade Granola

  • Back

      • Ingredients

    • Rolled oats
    • Honey
    • Nut butter (optional)
    • Chia seeds (optional)
    • Flax seeds (optional)
    • Chopped almonds (optional)
    • Pumpkin seeds (optional)
    • Coconut slices (optional)
    • Dried cranberries (optional)

      Process

    1. Mix oats, seeds, sliced nuts, and honey and/or nut butter together. It might get a bit sticky and you can always use your hands to mix them up.
    2. The texture should be sticky, but still spreadable.
    3. Toast them in a preheated oven at 180℃ for about 10 minutes.
    4. Onced baked, add in dried berries or chocolate chunks.

      Things to read before you start making this recipe

      This recipe is super forgiving and versatile. You can add in creative things to your own granola.

      Oats can get burnt easily, take them out of the oven once they change in color. (Don't worry, burnt oats still taste good)
