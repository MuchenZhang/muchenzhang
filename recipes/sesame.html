Sesame Crackers

  • Back

      • Ingredients

    • Flour 125g
    • Sesame seed 30g (Both black and white sesame seend work fine)
    • Salt 2g
    • Olive/corn/vegetable oil 15g
    • Water 70g

      Process

    1. Mix all the ingredients and form a dough.
    2. Cover and rest the dough for about 20 minutes to relax the gluten.
    3. Roll the dough out evenly and make it as thin as possible.
    4. Bake in a preheated oven at 150℃ for around 10 minutes.

      Things to read before you start making this recipe

      If your dough is too think, bake them under 150℃ for about 15 minutes and increase the temperature to 170℃ and bake for another 5 minutes.
