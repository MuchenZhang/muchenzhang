Cheese Sticks

  • Back

      • Ingredients

    • Mozzarella cheese, cut into strips
    • Some flour
    • 2 eggs, beaten
    • Some bread crumbs
    • Neutral oil for frying

      Process

    1. Cut the mozzarella cheese into strips.
    2. For each strip, coat it in flour, egg mixture, bread crumb, egg mixture, then bread crumb.
    3. Freeze the strips for 2 hours once they are coated to prevent leaking.
    4. Heat up oil to 180℃ and fry until golden brown.

      Things to read before you start making this recipe

      Try to use a type of cheese with low melting point if you prefer a soft, runny inside. I used Halloumi because that was the only cheese I can find at home, but it still turned out great.

      Season your bread crumbs if the cheese itself is flavorless.
