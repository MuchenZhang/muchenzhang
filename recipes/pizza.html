Homemade Pizza

  • Back

      • Ingredients

    • Flour 300g
    • Water 200mL
    • Salt 2g
    • Dry yeast 4g
    • Oilve oil 2 tbsp
    • Tomato sauce
    • Cheese
    • Toppings you have

      Process

    1. Mix the first five ingredients and form a dough. Knead the dough until smooth.
    2. Cover and rest the dough for one to two hours.
    3. Preheat the oven to 180℃.
    4. Stretch the dough to a circle/rectangle as thin as possible. Let it rest for 10 minutes.
    5. Spread the tomato sauce on and add your toppings.
    6. Bake for 15 minutes for a soft, gooey cheese on top or 20 minutes for a crispy cheese.

      Things to read before you start making this recipe

      Drizzle on some olive oil on the pizza before putting it in the oven for an extra flavor (I couldn't taste the difference though).

      If you want to have a more classic pizza, preheat the oven and a baking sheet to the highest temperature possible, slide the pizza in, and bake for 6 to 7 minutes.
