Pound Cake

  • Back

      • Ingredients

    • Softened unsalted butter 100g
    • Sugar 100g
    • 2 eggs
    • Flour 100g
    • Pinch of salt

      Process

    1. Cream the butter until lighter in color and fluffy.
    2. Add in sugar and mix until combined.
    3. Add in the eggs one at a time and mix until combined.
    4. Sift in flour and fold.
    5. Bake in a preheated oven at 170℃ for 50 to 55 minutes.

      Things to read before you start making this recipe

      Mix the butter A LOT, the cake will only rise if there is enough air beaten into the batter

      Pipe a thin line of butter in the middle before putting the batter into the oven to get the signature crack.
