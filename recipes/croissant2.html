Croissant 2.0

  • Back

      • Ingredients

    • Water 155g
    • Yeast 2g
    • Flour 330g
    • Sugar 20g
    • Salt 4g
    • Olive oil 20g
    • Butter 180g

      Process

    1. Mix every ingredients (except butter) together and knead till smooth.
    2. Cover and rest for 3 hours before transferring into the fridge and chill overnight.
    3. In the morning, slice the butter into equal parts and put it in a ziploc bag.
    4. Roll the butter out to a square. Refrigerate for 30 minutes or until the butter hardens.
    5. Take the dough out and roll it to a square, big enough to fit the butter.
    6. Place the butter slab 45 degrees relative to the square.
    7. Fold the dough in to cover the butter, pinch the sides.
    8. Rest in fridge for 30 minutes.
    9. Roll the dough out to a long rectangle and perform the "3 fold".
    10. Rest in fridge for 1 hour.
    11. Roll the dough out to a long rectangle and perform the "4 fold".
    12. Rest in fridge for 1 hour.
    13. Roll out to a thin rectangular sheet, mark out where to cut, and cut the dough into isosceles triangles.
    14. Roll the triangles and place them on a baking sheet.
    15. Brush on some egg wash (1 beaten egg) and let rise for 2 hours in room temp.
    16. Brush on another coat of egg wash before putting them in the oven.
    17. Bake at 220℃ for 10 miuntes and 190℃ for 14-17 minutes.
