Braided Loaf of Bread

  • Back

      • Ingredients

    • Flour 180g
    • Water 130g
    • Yeast 4g
    • Sugar 12g
    • Salt 2g
    • Butter 10g

      Process

    1. Mix up everything except the butter and mix until incorporated. Rest for 20 minutes.
    2. Add butter to the dough and mix well. Rest for another 20 minutes.
    3. Knead dough for three minutes until smooth.
    4. Prove for an hour or until doubled in size.
    5. Divide the dough into three equal portions. For each portion, roll out to a rectangle on a surface, then roll it up to a log.
    6. Braid the three logs just as if you are braiding hair.
    7. Tuck the ends under and put it in a loaf pan.
    8. Let it rise in the pan for another hour. Meanwhile, preheated oven to 190℃.
    9. Bake for 30 to 35 minutes.

      Things to read before you start making this recipe

      Be prepared for a workout if you don't have a dough mixer like me. The dough is very wet and might be hard to knead by hand, therefore, it is crucial to let the dough rest so gluten can form.
