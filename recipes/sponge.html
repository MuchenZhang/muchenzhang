Sponge Cake

  • Back

      • Ingredients

    • 5 eggs
    • Flour 70g
    • Oil 60mL
    • Water 50mL
    • Sugar 70g

      Process

    1. Separate the eggs into yolks and whites.
    2. Heat up oil to about 70℃, that's around 90 seconds in the microwave.
    3. Add the hot oil into sifted flour and mix.
    4. Add some water to cool down the temperature of the mixture.
    5. Gradually add all the yolks into the mixture and set aside.
    6. Preheat oven to 140℃.
    7. Beat the egg whites until soft peak. Gradually add in sugar during this process.
    8. Fold 1/3 of the beated egg whites into the yolk mixture until homogenous.
    9. Gently fold in the rest of the egg whites.
    10. Pour the final mixture into a cake mold. You can line some parchment paper under so you can pull out the cake as soon as it gets out the oven.
    11. Wrap the mold in foil if you are using one with a removable bottom.
    12. Place the mold on a tray filled half-way with room-temp water.
    13. Bake at 140℃ for one hour.
    14. Turn the temperature up to 150℃ for another 10 minutes for a darker color on top.
    15. Take it out of the oven and remove the cake out of the mold ASAP. Peel off the parchment paper too.
    16. Enjoy.

      Things to read before you start making this recipe

      Be gentle when folding the two mixtures.

      You can also use a non-stick mold, just make sure to get the cake out asap.

      I used a 9-inch mold and I think the cake turn out a bit too flat. Maybe switch out for a 6-inch mold.
