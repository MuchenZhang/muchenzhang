Scallion Pancake

  • Back

      • Ingredients

    • Flour 100g
    • Boiling water 50g
    • Cold water 10g
    • Chopped scallion 50g
    • Salt

      Process

    1. Add boiling water to the flour and mix. Then, add in the cold water and knead the dough until smooth.
    2. Cover the dough and rest for 30 minutes.
    3. Divide the dough into four portions. For each portion, shape into a ball with a smooth surface on top, brush with oil and rest for another 20 minutes.
    4. Roll the dough out into a 20*5cm rectangle.
    5. Sprinkle on some salt and scallion.
    6. Roll the rectangle up from its longer side, you will get a log that is 20cm long.
    7. Tightly seal the loose points and then roll the log up into a snail-like shape. Tuck the end behind.
    8. Rest for 5 minutes and squish the "snail" down.
    9. Roll it out into a disk with spirals on it.
    10. Heat up an oiled pan and cook them over medium heat. Remember to flip frequently.

      Things to read before you start making this recipe

      It is very important to rest the dough to allow gluten development.

      You can always freeze the raw pancakes and cook them in the morning for breakfast.
