Cream Puffs

  • Back

      • Ingredients

    • butter 35g
    • milk 160mL
    • 3 eggs
    • flour 50g
    • a pinch of salt
    • sugar 20g
    • whipped cream

      Process

    1. Preheat oven to 200℃.
    2. Add the butter and milk to a saucepan, bring it to a simmer over medium high heat, then add in sugar and salt.
    3. Bring the mixture to a boil and add in flour.
    4. Over low heat, combine the liquid and flour.
    5. Turn off heat, let the dough-like mixture cool for 2 minutes.
    6. Add in 1 egg at a time and mix.
    7. The dough should be ready when a triangle forms on the spatula.
    8. Pipe the dough into circles on a parchment paper.
    9. Brush on eggwash to make the puffs glossy.
    10. Bake at 200℃ for about 15 minutes, without opening the oven, bake for another 20 minutes at 170℃.
    11. Onced baked, pipe in the cream.

      Things to read before you start making this recipe

      Add one egg at a time. The mixture should look glossy at first, but it will return to a sticky dough once it's combined with the egg.

      Never open the oven door, heat will go away and the cream puffs ain't no gonna be puffy.
