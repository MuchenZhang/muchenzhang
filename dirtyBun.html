Dirty Bun 脏脏包

  • Back

      • Ingredients

      Dough

    • flour 250g
    • cocoa powder 10g
    • cold milk 150mL
    • yeast 3g
    • 1 egg
    • salt 1 tsp
    • sugar 1tbsp
    • softened butter 10g

      • Chocolate Ganache

    • 100g dark chocolate
    • 150g heavy cream
    • some cocoa powder for the top

      Process

    1. Mix up all ingredients except for butter and knead until smooth.
    2. Add in butter and knead. The dough should preferably pass the windowpane test.
    3. Once the dough is nice and smooth, cover and rest in the fridge for 30 minutes.
    4. Put about 100g of cold butter in a plastic bag or a piece of parchment paper.
    5. Roll out the butter into a rectangle and freeze for 20 minutes.
    6. Once the butter is cold and hard again, take the dough out and roll it out into a rectangle 1.5 times the size of the butter.
    7. Place the butter in the center of the dough and close the dough up. We are making a pastry now, it is the same as making a croissant.
    8. Put the closed dough in the freezer for another 20 minutes.
    9. Repeat these two steps for another three times.
    10. Take out the dough and roll it out to a bigger, thinner sheet.
    11. Divide the dough into stripes
    12. Place a piece of chocolate at the beginning and roll.
    13. Once the individual little buns are rolled up, cover loosely with plastic wrap and let rise for 1 hour.
    14. Bake in preheated oven at 180℃ for 20 minutes. We are going to make chocolate ganache.
    15. Add the chocolate and heavy cream in a bowl and microwave in 30 sec intervals. Stir after each interval.
    16. Let the ganache cool a little and dip the top of the bun in it.
    17. Sift some cocoa powder on top and you got a calorie bomb.

      Things to read before you start making this recipe

      Remember to rest the buns at a cool temperature before baking since butter WILL melt at 305K.
