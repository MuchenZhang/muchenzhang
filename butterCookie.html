3-Ingredients Butter Cookies

  • Back

      • Ingredients

    • Unsalted Butter softened 50g
    • Sugar 40g
    • Flour 125g

      Process

    1. Mix up butter, sugar, and flour.
    2. Press it into a dough and refrigerate it for 30 minutes.
    3. Roll the dough out and cut into circles. You can use the rim of a cup for it.
    4. Bake in a preheated oven at 160℃ for 15 minutes.
    5. Let cool and serve.

      Things to read before you start making this recipe

      You can make them into chocolate or matcha flavored cookies by adding 7g of cocoa powder or matcha powder into the dough.

      You can decorate this cookie by creating imprints on them with a cookie stamp. I tried creating some patterns using the sides of a whiskey glass but it didn't show on the cookies once they came out of the oven. You can freeze the dough before making imprints so the dough hardens up and makes the pattern show.
